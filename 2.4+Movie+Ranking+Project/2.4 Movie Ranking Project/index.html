Top Movies of all time by Akshat Khandelwal

My best 3 movies.

Inception (2010) - Directed by Christopher Nolan

I love "Inception" for its complex narrative structure, mind-bending concept, and exceptional execution. The film delves into the depths of the subconscious mind, exploring themes of reality, dreams, and perception. The visual effects are stunning, especially the scenes that defy gravity and physics.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994) - Directed by Frank Darabont

The film tells the story of hope, resilience, and the human spirit through the experiences of Andy Dufresne, a wrongly convicted man, and his friendship with fellow inmate Red. The narrative is beautifully crafted, with rich character development and poignant moments that tug at the heartstrings.

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy (2001-2003) - Directed by Peter Jackson

The scale and scope of the story, coupled with breathtaking visuals and groundbreaking special effects, make "The Lord of the Rings" an unforgettable cinematic experience. Beyond the epic battles and fantastical landscapes, the heart of the trilogy lies in its characters and their journey of courage, friendship, and sacrifice.
